# Kitemarking Initiative (Internal Quality)

**Strategic Objective:** 6-12 month focus on rolling out the Kitemarking initiative (Deloitte internal Quality assessments).
**Owner:** Stephen McMaster (Partner, UK Engineering CTO)

## Key Personnel
*   **Conor (Chief of Staff):** Full-time dedication. Currently tasked with working through the commercial/billing model for the Kitemarking process.
*   **Tony (Initiative Lead):** Assigned to lead the overall initiative. He has been given dedicated "free time" to execute this, and it is tied directly to his career objectives.

## Scope & Rollout Strategy
*   **Initial Target:** Focusing exclusively on projects with >£10m billables to keep the assessment volume manageable.
*   **Action Item:** The team has been tasked with providing an effort-based view of the work required to execute these initial assessments.
*   **External Dependencies:** Significant bids landing (currently awaiting a large one) could derail capacity. However, strong top-down executive sponsorship is anticipated to mitigate this friction.

## Current Strategic Friction & Risks
*   **Capacity & Incentives:** While Tony has dedicated time, the broader pool of architects required to perform the Kitemarking assessments do not. Until Conor finalises a billing model, this remains a "side-of-desk" activity for them.
*   **Action Item:** Stephen needs to develop a strategy to secure the wider architect pool's time and mandate their participation without overloading their billable client commitments in the interim.
