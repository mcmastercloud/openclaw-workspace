# Stephen McMaster: Operating Rhythm & Capacity Strategy

## Core Objectives
*   **Target Working Cadence:** Early rise to an 8:00pm/9:00pm hard cut-off on work days.
*   **Primary Constraint:** Deep sleep, consistent energy levels, and protecting strategic thinking time from administrative drag.
*   **Current Reality:** Frequently fatigued, inconsistent morning starts, and bogged down in tactical/confidential email management.

## Proposed Daily Architecture (Draft)
*This is the framework we need to pressure-test and refine.*

1.  **Phase 1: Physiological Foundation (Pre-Work)**
    *   *Purpose:* Prime the system for an extended 12+ hour cognitive load.
    *   *Action:* Mandatory exercise/movement block to ensure physical fatigue for deep sleep later.

2.  **Phase 2: Deep Strategic Execution (Mornings)**
    *   *Purpose:* Capitalise on natural high engagement and cognitive clarity.
    *   *Action:* Uninterrupted thinking time (e.g., Kitemarking strategy, CTO operating models). *No inbox access allowed during this block.*

3.  **Phase 3: Tactical & Collaborative Execution (Mid-Day to Afternoon)**
    *   *Purpose:* High-bandwidth interactions, team alignment, and unblocking others.
    *   *Action:* Meetings, working sessions with Conor (Chief of Staff), and project reviews.

4.  **Phase 4: Administrative Containment (Late Afternoon/Evening)**
    *   *Purpose:* Process the confidential inbox and clear the decks for the next day.
    *   *Action:* Dedicated, time-boxed administrative block.

5.  **Phase 5: The Hard Disconnect (8:00pm - 9:00pm)**
    *   *Purpose:* System recovery.
    *   *Action:* Strict shut-down sequence. No work-related inputs beyond the cut-off to protect sleep quality.
    *   *Context:* This extended workday is feasible because Stephen frequently travels for work during the week, meaning the extended hours do not cannibalise time with his Partner (Samuel).
    *   *Enforcement Mechanism:* A non-negotiable 9:00pm gym session (weights or treadmill) acts as the artificial circuit breaker when staying in hotels, enforcing the end of the working day and initiating physiological recovery.
    *   *Exception Handling:* Team dinners and client engagements frequently disrupt this evening discipline. The operating model must adapt to ensure physical exertion and the 9:00pm cutoff are protected even on days with evening commitments. When evening commitments exist, the gym session must be pulled forward to a 06:00am start to guarantee execution.
